History of languages

We know that it all started with sounds, that somehow started to make sense over time.

Different peoples, produced different sounds, and evolved into languages over millennia.

Main ingredients of a language

Our default soup is made with the following:

  • 2 scoops of misplaced vowels
  • 3½ dangling participles, shaken not stirred
  • 1 jar of consonants in brine
  • A sack of silent letters (you never use them, but they must be there)
